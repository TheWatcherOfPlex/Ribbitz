## üê∏ Grung (Homebrew v4) ‚Äî Racial Traits

> Source: **Grung Frog Folk Homebrew ‚Äì Quick Start Guide v4**

### Frog Eyes
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- Eyes are usually the same color as the frog, but turn a glowing red or green if alignment drops to evil.

### Ability Score Increase
- DEX +2, CON +1 (already applied to Ribbitz totals).

### Arboreal Alertness
- Proficiency in **Perception**.

### Jumping
Your jump total includes both vertical and horizontal movement.

- **Long Jump (running start):** `d20 + (STR or DEX) + Proficiency`
- **Standing Jump:** `(d20 + (STR or DEX) + Proficiency) / 2`

### Amphibious
You can breathe air and water.

- **Fresh water:** can remain submerged up to **1 hour** before surfacing.
- **Saltwater:** can remain submerged up to **30 minutes** before surfacing.
  - Must wash off the salt before breathing while submerged again in salt water.

### Bite
- Natural weapon. **Piercing** damage: `1d6 + STR mod`.

### Tongue Slap
- Natural weapon. Uses the same hit bonus as an unarmed strike.
- **Piercing** damage: `1d6 + STR mod`.

### Tongue Grapple
- Use tongue to grapple and pull an enemy up to **10 ft**.
- Target makes a **DEX saving throw** against your **STR (Athletics)** check.
- **Uses:** PB per long rest.

### Camouflage
- Your skin adapts to match surroundings, granting proficiency in **Stealth**.

### Poisons (Skin / Saliva)
- Any creature that grapples you or comes into direct contact with your skin or saliva must succeed on a CON save: **DC = 12 + PB**.
- On fail: **Poisoned** for **1 minute**.
- A poisoned creature no longer in contact with you can repeat the save at the end of each turn, ending the effect on a success.

#### Poison Color Effects
- **Green:** Restricts movement.
- **Blue:** Forces victim to shout/yelp.
- **Purple:** Drives victim to desperately seek water.
- **Red:** Induces ravenous hunger.
- **Orange:** Causes irrational fears of friends.
- **Gold:** Charms victim and grants ability to speak Grung.

### Poison Immunity
- You are immune to **poison damage** and the **poisoned** condition.

### Poison Weapon
- As a **bonus action**, apply your Grung color poison to any weapon.
- Target must succeed on a save: **DC = 9 + PB + CON mod**.
- **Uses:** PB per day.
- Requirement: you must have submerged in water or moistened your skin within the last 24 hours.

### Race Weaknesses ‚Äî Water Dependency
- You must immerse yourself in water for at least **1 hour** (or moisturize) every **24 hours** or gain **1 level of exhaustion**.
- Exhaustion can only be removed through magic, or by immersing in water for 1 hour, or moisturizing.
